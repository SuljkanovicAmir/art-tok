# ArtTok — Redesign Brief

## What is ArtTok

A TikTok-style app for discovering museum art. Users swipe through full-screen artwork cards, save favorites, search, and browse by culture/classification. Dark theme, mobile-first.

**Screens:** Feed (vertical scroll), Search (grid), Liked (collection), Browse (categories), Artwork Detail.

---

## Vision: "Museum at Night"

The UI feels like polished gallery glass in a dark museum. The only light comes from the artworks — their colors bleed softly through every glass surface. The result is immersive, cinematic, and premium.

**References:** TikTok / Instagram Reels layout, iOS 26 Liquid Glass / Apple Vision Pro aesthetic, editorial museum catalog typography.

**Mood:** Dark, luminous, immersive. The art is always the hero — the UI is the frame, never the focus.

---

## Design Pillars

1. **Glassmorphism everywhere** — Bold, not subtle. Heavy backdrop blur, luminous borders, specular highlights, subtle noise grain. Multiple glass elevation tiers for visual hierarchy (lighter glass for backgrounds, denser glass for interactive elements).

2. **Per-artwork accent color** — Each artwork generates its own accent hue. This tints the glass surfaces, glows, and borders on that card. No two screens look the same. There is no fixed brand color — the artwork IS the color.

3. **Serif meets sans** — Editorial serif for art-facing text (titles, headings, brand). Clean geometric sans for UI text (labels, body, metadata). The pairing says "high art meets modern tech."

4. **Motion with purpose** — Staggered reveals when content enters, breathing glows that invite interaction, smooth spring transitions. Everything feels alive but never distracting.

5. **Floating, not anchored** — Navigation is a floating glass pill, not an edge-to-edge bar. Elements feel like they hover over the content rather than being bolted to the frame.

---

## Key Interactions

- **Feed:** Full-bleed artwork with a compact glass info strip at the bottom and floating glass action buttons on the right. Double-tap to like with an accent-colored heart burst.
- **Expanded details:** Glass bottom sheet slides up over the artwork (artwork stays visible behind the frost) rather than pushing content down.
- **Grid pages:** Glass cards with staggered entrance animations and hover lift effects.
- **Categories:** Top categories as larger hero glass cards, remaining as size-varied glass pills (popular = bigger).
